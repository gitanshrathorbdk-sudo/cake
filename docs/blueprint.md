# **App Name**: Harmonica

## Core Features:

- Music Playback: Play and pause music, skip to the next or previous track, and manage the play queue.
- Music Library: Display uploaded tracks, with a filter bar.
- Playlist Creation - Manual: Allows users to manually create and manage playlists by selecting songs from their library.
- AI Playlist Generation: Leverages AI to automatically generate playlists based on user-defined parameters like song characteristics and mood. This AI powered tool analyzes music characteristics to create playlists.
- Local Music Upload: Enables users to upload music files from their device, populating editable song metadata fields.
- Offline Mode: Allows users to play uploaded music even without an internet connection.
- Online Sync: Synchronizes music library and playlist data across devices when online.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark theme with a primary color of deep grayish-blue (#3B4463) for a modern and sophisticated look. It should visually recede to create better focus on the content.
- Accent color of mustard gold (#E6B422) to provide visual contrast and highlight interactive elements, call to actions and status.
- Background color: a slightly lighter shade of gray-blue (#4A557A) to offer subtle contrast without overpowering the primary color.
- Font: 'Inter', a sans-serif typeface, to ensure clear, consistent readability in both headlines and body text.
- Home screen featuring Harmonica logo, a 4x3 grid of music labels, a music control bar at the bottom, and a scrollable view for listening stats.
- A 'Create a playlist' button, with 'Make by Yourself' and 'By AI' pop-up options, maintaining a balanced, accessible user interface.
- Use modern and minimalist icons for music controls, playlist actions, and navigation to create a consistent and intuitive user experience.
